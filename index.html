ABOUT US

Welcome to the Holiday Lodge & Conference Center

Off Route 19, this unpretentious hotel is a 5-minute walk from the White Oak Rail Trail and 10 miles from the New River Gorge Bridge. Located in near the New River National Park

Down-to-earth rooms have, tea and coffeemakers, minifridges and high-speed Internet access. Connecting rooms are available as well as a fitness center, & buffet breakfast all included in the price of your room.
